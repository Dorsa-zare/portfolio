Spatial Composition and Player Progression

Level Layout Study

Date of completion: 2026

Tools: Unreal Engine 5

Role: Environment Design, Level Layout

Focus: Pacing, navigation, visual composition

Design Goal

This study explored how layered sightlines, monumental forms, and path composition can shape pacing and curiosity through an environment. The goal was to create progression that feels readable while still inviting exploration.

Spatial Composition Direction

The environment was structured around visible landmarks and staged reveals, using architecture to establish rhythm between approach, anticipation, and movement. Scale was used as both an emotional device and a navigation tool.

The project focused on how players read space over time, using composition to pull attention forward and clarify where progress can happen next.
