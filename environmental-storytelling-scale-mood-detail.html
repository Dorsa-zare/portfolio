Environmental Storytelling Through Scale and Mood

Game Design Exploration

Date of completion: 2026

Tools: Unreal Engine 5

Role: Game Design, Spatial Storytelling

Focus: Atmosphere, orientation, emotional tone

Design Goal

This exploration examined how atmosphere, landmarks, and environmental contrast can communicate narrative weight while still supporting player orientation. The goal was to let mood and spatial storytelling work together rather than compete.

Environmental Storytelling Direction

The space was shaped around contrast in scale, density, and visual framing to create emotional shifts as players moved through it. Landmarks were positioned to anchor navigation while atmosphere carried the larger narrative tone.

This project focused on using environment as both a storytelling surface and a guide for player attention, helping the world communicate meaning without relying heavily on explicit exposition.

Project Snapshot
